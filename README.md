# 🧬 Poofie

### Discover Your Developer DNA.

Poofie is an AI-powered developer identity platform that helps developers understand themselves beyond résumés, coding profiles, and social media.

Today's developers leave traces of their journey across dozens of platforms. GitHub captures their code, LeetCode measures problem-solving ability, Devfolio records hackathon participation, and LinkedIn attempts to summarize everything into a professional profile. Each platform tells a piece of the story, but none of them reveal the complete picture.

Poofie brings these fragmented signals together to create a living developer identity.

By analyzing a developer's activity across platforms such as GitHub, LeetCode, LinkedIn, and Devfolio, alongside personality and interest-based assessments, Poofie generates a unique Developer DNA that reflects how they learn, build, collaborate, and grow.

---

## 🚀 Why Poofie?

The internet has made it easier than ever to showcase achievements, but surprisingly difficult to answer simple questions:

* What kind of developer am I?
* What technologies genuinely suit me?
* Who would I work well with?
* Where can I find people like me?
* How do I discover communities that match my interests?

Most platforms focus on showcasing work.

Poofie focuses on understanding the person behind the work.

## 🧠 Developer DNA

At the heart of Poofie is the concept of Developer DNA.

Instead of categorizing people through job titles, Poofie identifies behavioral patterns and technical tendencies to determine how a developer naturally approaches technology.

Examples include:

* ⚒️ Maker
* 🏛️ Architect
* 🧭 Explorer
* ♟️ Strategist
* 📚 Scholar
* ⚗️ Alchemist
* ⚡ Catalyst
* 💎 Craftsman

Developer DNA is generated using a combination of platform activity, technical interests, project history, and personality-driven assessments.

The result is a profile that feels personal, understandable, and genuinely representative of the developer.

---

## 🔍 Proof Over Claims

Poofie is built around a simple principle:

**Evidence is more valuable than self-description.**

Rather than relying solely on bios, titles, certificates, or self-declared expertise, Poofie evaluates verifiable activity across platforms.

The system analyzes:

* GitHub repositories and contributions
* LeetCode progress
* Hackathon participation
* Project history
* Open source involvement
* Technical consistency

This information contributes to a developer's Proof Index, a credibility layer designed to prioritize demonstrated work over buzzwords.

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. Connect developer platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, LeetCode, and Devfolio.
2. Complete the Poofie Developer DNA assessment.
3. Let Poofie's AI analyze technical activity and behavioral patterns.
4. Generate a complete developer identity profile.
5. Discover communities, collaborators, and opportunities tailored to you.
